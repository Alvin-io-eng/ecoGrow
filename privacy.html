Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Data & Trust

ECO GROW is committed to responsible data handling, user privacy and transparent information practices for all visitors and supporters.

Information Collection

We collect only the personal information you provide directly, such as name, email address and contact details when you subscribe, volunteer or partner with ECO GROW.

Use of Information

Your data is used to communicate program updates, manage event participation and deliver relevant news about conservation initiatives. We do not sell or trade your personal information.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and analytics only to improve navigation and measure engagement. You can manage option preferences through your browser settings.

Security

We maintain reasonable safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure or misuse.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, email us at privacy@ecogrow.org.
